ABOUT US

Art is not a result of coincidence, but rather a product of talent and study in several fields, including the Turkish language, culture, and their specific influence on Islamic art. The study of interior design also emphasizes Islamic art in architecture and decor. What sets us apart is our awareness of the importance of Arabic calligraphy. Therefore, we study it in detail in various forms, including Thuluth, Naskh, Ruq'ah, Diwani, Persian, and Islamic ornamentation. All of this study converges towards one goal: showcasing the beauty of Arabic calligraphy.
